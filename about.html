Our Organization

ADOMUHA SAFETY CONSCIOUSNESS FOUNDATION (ASCF) is a non-governmental organization established in the year 2020, registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), dedicated to promoting safety awareness and education.

We carry out this function by empowering secondary school students with the knowledge and skills necessary to prevent and respond to safety threats, thereby reducing security illiteracy in our communities.

We have successfully delivered impactful safety awareness seminars to numerous secondary schools in Abuja, Nasarawa State, and Lokoja, promoting a culture of safety and responsibility among young minds.

Recently, we launched a safety awareness guidebook with a target of distributing 60,000 copies to 300 schools across Kogi State. This grand initiative is aimed at reaching vulnerable youth in both schools and communities to eradicate safety illiteracy and reduce exposure to crime.

Collaboration and sponsorship are essential to the success of this project. With the right partnerships, we can amplify our safety message and make a long-lasting impact on the next generation.

This initiative has the potential to significantly transform the safety landscape of Kogi State, promoting safety literacy and strengthening resilience among young people.

Our Values

Safety

We prioritize safety in all aspects of our work, ensuring that our programs and activities promote a culture of safety and responsibility.

Education

We believe that education is key to promoting safety awareness and empowering individuals with the knowledge and skills to prevent inherent dangers.

Community

We are committed to working with communities to promote safety awareness and education, recognizing that community engagement is essential.

Empowerment

We empower individuals and communities with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to take control of their safety and well-being.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration and partnership, working with stakeholders from all sectors to promote safety awareness.

Respect for the Law

Everything we undertake is grounded in our adherence to the ASCF's Constitution and compliance with Nigeria laws and regulations.

Integrity

We operate with integrity, transparency, and accountability, ensuring that our programs are of the highest quality and impact.

Respect

We respect the dignity and worth of every individual, recognizing that safety is a fundamental human right.
