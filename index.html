About ASCF

Adomuha Safety Consciousness Foundation (ASCF) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting safety awareness and education. We empower students and individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to prevent and respond to safety threats.

Learn More

Our Mission

To empower students, individuals and communities with awareness, education, and skill, promoting a culture of safety, responsibility, and resilience.

Our Vision

Empowering students with practical safety knowledge to reduce victimhood and promote a culture of safety consciousness.

Seasonal Activities

Adomuha Safety Consciousness Foundation conducts yearly structured activities to promote safety awareness, educate the public, and foster collaboration with stakeholders.

Quarter 1 (January - April)

  • New Year Safety Resolutions: Distribute safety tips and resources for individuals and families to prioritize safety in the new year (January)
  • Fire Safety Awareness Month (February)
  • Cyber Safety Awareness Month (March)
  • Road Safety Week: Organize workshops, seminars, or social media campaigns to raise awareness about road safety and promote safe driving practices (April)

Quarter 2 (May - June)

  • Charity Football Match: Raise awareness and funds for safety initiatives (May)
  • Online Fundraising Campaigns: Expand donor reach and engagement through digital channels

Quarter 3 (July - December)

  • Back-to-School Safety: Distribute safety guides for students, parents, and teachers, covering school safety, bullying prevention, and emergency response
  • Community Outreach Programs: Organize safety education and awareness campaigns within communities
  • Strategic Partnerships: Network with other organizations for joint safety initiatives

Educational Programs (September - November)

  • Safety Guidebook Distribution: Deliver printed materials and hold awareness sessions in schools
