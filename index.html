About ASCF

Adomuha Safety Consciousness Foundation (ASCF) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting safety awareness and education. We empower students and individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to prevent and respond to safety threats.

Learn More

Our Mission

To empower students, individuals and communities with awareness, education, and skill, promoting a culture of safety, responsibility, and resilience.

Our Vision

Empowering students with practical safety knowledge to reduce victimhood and promote a culture of safety consciousness.
