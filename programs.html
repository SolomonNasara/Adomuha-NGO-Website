Our Programs

Discover our initiatives to promote safety awareness and education

School Safety Program

Our flagship program focuses on educating secondary school students about various safety issues they may encounter in their daily lives. Through interactive workshops and seminars, we cover topics such as:

  • Personal safety and awareness
  • Crime prevention strategies
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Cyber safety and online security
  • Bullying prevention

We have reached over 50 schools across Abuja, Nasarawa State, and Lokoja, impacting thousands of students with vital safety knowledge.

Community Safety Initiative

This program extends our safety education to communities, working with local leaders and residents to create safer neighborhoods. Activities include:

  • Community safety workshops
  • Neighborhood watch training
  • Crime prevention seminars
  • Emergency preparedness sessions
  • Safety awareness campaigns

By engaging entire communities, we create a culture of collective responsibility for safety and security.

Youth Empowerment Program

Recognizing that empowered youth are less vulnerable to crime and violence, this program provides:

  • Leadership training with safety focus
  • Skill acquisition workshops
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Peer educator training
  • Career guidance sessions

We equip young people with both safety knowledge and life skills to help them make positive choices.

Victim Support Services

For those who have experienced crime or safety threats, we offer:

  • Crisis counseling
  • Legal guidance referrals
  • Support groups
  • Rehabilitation assistance
  • Empowerment programs

Our goal is to help victims recover and regain their sense of security and confidence.

Our Core Objectives
